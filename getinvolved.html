Join Our Core Team

At Ziraal, our mission is powered by passionate individuals dedicated to making a difference. We are always looking for talented and committed volunteers to join our core team across various departments. While applications are currently closed, you can learn more about our key areas of work below.

Marketing

Help us spread the word about Ziraal's initiatives. This department focuses on digital campaigns, content creation, social media management, and public relations to reach a wider audience and engage supporters.

Outreach

Connect with communities and build relationships. This team organizes events, manages volunteer recruitment, and facilitates direct engagement with beneficiaries and local partners.

Operations

Ensure the smooth functioning of our programs. This department handles project management, resource allocation, administrative tasks, and logistical support for all our initiatives.

Fundraising & Partnerships

Drive our financial sustainability. This team focuses on grant writing, donor relations, corporate partnerships, and organizing fundraising campaigns to secure vital resources.

Volunteer With Ziraal

Student Power. Real Impact.

Ziraal is a student-led humanitarian project driven by the belief that everyone deserves dignity, access, and opportunity. Our work focuses on providing educational support, food initiatives, and essential community outreach to underprivileged urban populations. We need passionate volunteers who are ready to help us build a more compassionate and equitable societyâ€”one action at a time.

Why Volunteer With Ziraal?

  • Make a tangible difference in urban communities
  • Join a youth-led, action-driven team
  • Gain hands-on experience in humanitarian service
  • Build meaningful skills in logistics, media, education, and outreach
  • Be part of a community of changemakers

Volunteer Roles

Explore where your skills can shine:

Educational Support

  • Tutor school-age children
  • Assist in creating learning materials
  • Organize or lead community classes/workshops

Food Initiatives

  • Pack and distribute meals
  • Organize food collection or drives
  • Support meal planning and outreach

Logistics

  • Coordinate donations, materials, and volunteers
  • Support event setup and delivery operations
  • Help with inventory and distribution systems

Fundraising

  • Help plan crowdfunding campaigns
  • Reach out to sponsors and donors
  • Create promotional content for giving initiatives

Photography & Videography

  • Document our impact through visuals
  • Cover Ziraal events and activities
  • Create media content for social platforms

Community Outreach

  • Help connect Ziraal with communities in need
  • Spread awareness through word-of-mouth and events
  • Distribute flyers, organize meetings, build trust

How to Get Started

  1. Apply Now: Fill out our quick volunteer form.
  2. Meet the Team: Join a short introduction session to get you started.
  3. Join a Role: Get matched based on your skills and availability.
  4. Create Impact: Be part of real, on-the-ground change.
